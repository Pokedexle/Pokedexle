# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Pokedexle community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all.
We are committed to fostering an environment that respects the dignity, rights, and contributions of all people, regardless of characteristics including race, ethnicity, caste, color, age, physical characteristics, neurodiversity, disability, sex or gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, language, philosophy or religion, national or social origin, socio-economic position, level of education, or other status.

Participation privileges are extended to everyone who engages in good faith and in accordance with this Code of Conduct.

## Encouraged Behaviors

We strive to:

1. Respect the purpose of this project, its activities, and its collaboration spaces.
2. Engage kindly, constructively, and honestly with others.
3. Respect different viewpoints and lived experiences.
4. Take responsibility for our actions and contributions.
5. Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
6. Repair harm when it occurs.
7. Behave in ways that support the long-term well-being of the community.

## Restricted Behaviors

The following behaviors are prohibited. Instances, threats, or promotion of these behaviors are violations of this Code of Conduct:

1. Harassment, including violating clearly expressed personal boundaries.
2. Character attacks, including insulting, demeaning, or pejorative comments toward individuals or groups.
3. Discrimination or stereotyping based on identity or protected characteristics.
4. Sexualized behavior or conduct that is inappropriately intimate for the project context.
5. Violating confidentiality by sharing personal or private information without permission.
6. Endangerment, including encouraging or threatening violence or harm.
7. Any behavior that undermines the safety or well-being of the community.

### Other Restrictions

1. Impersonating another person, including to evade enforcement actions.
2. Failing to credit sources for contributed content where attribution is expected.
3. Sharing promotional or commercial content outside normal project expectations.
4. Irresponsibly sharing content that includes or promotes restricted behaviors.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including repository discussions, pull requests, reviews, and other official communication channels.
It also applies when someone is officially representing the project in public spaces (for example, using an official project contact or acting as an appointed representative).

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently.
They may take any action they deem appropriate to protect the community, including moderating or removing content, limiting participation, or restricting access to project spaces.

## Addressing and Repairing Harm

When a report is validated, maintainers may apply the following enforcement ladder, depending on impact and severity. Lower steps may be skipped when necessary.

1. Warning
    - Event: A single incident or a low-severity violation.
    - Consequence: Private written warning.
    - Repair: Acknowledgment, clarification, and corrective behavior.

2. Temporarily Limited Activities
    - Event: Repeated behavior after a warning, or a more serious first incident.
    - Consequence: Temporary limits on specific interactions or channels.
    - Repair: Reflection period, corrective action, and respectful re-engagement.

3. Temporary Suspension
    - Event: Repeated violations or a serious violation affecting community safety.
    - Consequence: Temporary suspension from project spaces.
    - Repair: Compliance with return conditions and demonstrated behavior change.

4. Permanent Ban
    - Event: Severe or repeated violations where safety cannot otherwise be ensured.
    - Consequence: Permanent removal from project participation spaces.
    - Repair: Not applicable at this level.
